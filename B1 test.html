B1 English Grammar Placement Test

Complete the questions below to check your B1 intermediate grammar level.

Part 1: Multiple Choice

1. I ___ dinner when the lights suddenly ___ out. (Past Continuous)

2. She ___ Paris three times in her life. (Present Perfect)

3. You look absolutely exhausted! ___ all day? (Present Perfect Continuous)

4. If I ___ the umbrella, I __ completely wet! (3rd Conditional)

5. They ___ the exam if they ___ harder. (3rd Conditional)

6. I strongly believe that ___ health is more important than ___ money. (Advanced Articles)

7. I really can't stand ___ in long queues. (Gerund / Infinitive)

8. She promised ___ me with my difficult project. (Gerund / Infinitive)

9. I ___ my keys yesterday, but I ___ them yet. (Present Perfect)

10. We decided to go to ___ Alps for our winter holiday. (Articles)

Part 2: Sentence Transformation & Fill in the blanks

11. Вставьте глагол в Present Perfect Continuous:
I started reading this book two weeks ago and I haven't finished it. -> I ____________________ this book for two weeks.

12. Закончите предложение (Conditional):
She was late because she missed the bus. -> If she hadn't missed the bus, she ____________________ late.

13. Раскройте скобки (Gerund / Infinitive). Напишите только одно слово:
Do you mind (close) the door? It's freezing!

14. Вставьте правильную форму:
___ (you / finish) your homework yet?
